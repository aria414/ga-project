Introduction


This is a visual novel project that is playable online. Story is completely made up. Anything that may resemble actual places, people or things is coincidental.

A visual novel is a game that is heavily story based. Players read through the stories and are often asked to select a choice. Selecting certain choices will make the story branch of to another scenario, or take them to a different "route". Different routes may have completely different ending. Sometimes players have to get a certian number of choices correct to unlock a happy ending. Othertimes they unlock the normal ending.

  • Click on the arrow button on the bottom right corner to continue reading text.
  • When a choice pops up, click to choose one.
  • *Warning, choosing the wrong choice may lead to a bad end.
  • Choosing the right choice will let you continue with the story.
